# EdenRank - AI Citation OS

**The problem:** AI assistants mention brands far more often than they cite them as a source. A mention is invisible credit; a citation is a link, traffic, and trust. Most tools do not even measure the difference.

**EdenRank measures both - and then earns the missing citations.**

## What it does

- **Tracks real buyer prompts** across 10 AI answer engines: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Google AI Mode, Microsoft Copilot, Gemini, Grok, Mistral, DeepSeek, You.com
- **Separates mentions from citations** per prompt, per engine - every metric ships with its sample size, never a bare percentage
- **Citation Source Graph** - maps which domains AI engines actually cite in your niche, from measured runs, not guesses
- **Citable-gap targeting** - finds the prompts where engines already cite somebody (so a citation is provably winnable) but never you
- **Diagnostics** - AI-crawler access, robots.txt, llms.txt, schema.org, entity checks
- **Action engine** - content briefs, owned answer pages, outreach drafts; approval-first: nothing leaves the system without an explicit human OK
- **Verification** - before/after windows with real statistical tests (Fisher exact); the system reports "no effect" honestly instead of inventing wins
- **Weekly reports** with receipts: what was measured, what was done, what changed - zeros stay visible
